# Incident Log

Historical record of significant errors and resolutions.

---

## Log Format

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD: Brief Title

**Symptoms:** What was observed
**Root Cause:** Why it happened
**Solution:** How it was fixed
**Prevention:** How to avoid in the future
```
